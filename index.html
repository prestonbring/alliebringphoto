About Me

Hi! I'm Lexi Kostelecky, a North Dakota-based photographer with a passion for capturing life's special moments. Born and raised in Dickinson, North Dakota, I now find myself in Fargo while I pursue my education at North Dakota State University. As a 21-year-old junior majoring in Photography, I delight in mixing my academic learning with my creative instincts to produce stunning images.



A little about me: I specialize in portraits, couples, weddings, and other fun events. Whether it's the joy of your special day or capturing you at your best, I strive to capture the essence of every moment. Photography isn't just a hobby for me; it’s an art form that helps me connect with and celebrate the beauty around us. I find joy in freezing time and turning fleeting moments into lasting memories.


My passion for the arts runs deep. I've been dedicated to professional dance for a decade, and I also play several instruments, including the violin, guitar, and piano. I love reading and enjoy painting, always seeking new ways to express my creativity. This journey has led me to my latest chapter: fulfilling my passion for art through photography. I see my work as a form of worship for my Creator ✟, capturing the beauty He has bestowed upon the world.




Beyond my camera lens, my faith is a cornerstone of my life. I'm a proud Catholic, and you'll often find me at St. Paul's Newman Center just off campus, where I've found a second home. It's my go-to place for reflection, community, and spiritual growth. If you're looking for me and I'm not behind my camera, there's a good chance you'll spot me there along with my many close friends and wonderful fiancé!


When I'm not studying or shooting photos, I love to immerse myself in the beauty of everyday life. I'm a big believer in the magic of ordinary moments – a walk around campus, a coffee with friends, or a quiet evening at home. These little slices of life often inspire my work and remind me of the beauty that surrounds us every day.


Why do I do what I do? Because I believe that every person has a unique story to tell, and I feel honored to be able to tell that story through my photographs. My goal is to make every photo session fun, relaxed, and memorable. I want you to feel comfortable and to let your true essence shine through in every shot.



So, whether you're looking for someone to capture your wedding day, a family portrait, or just some beautiful shots of your favorite places, I'm here for you! ♡ Let's create something beautiful together!

I'm excited to get to know you and help you capture the moments that matter most. Feel free to reach out, and let's make some magic happen!

FAQ & Investment

I’m excited to work with you and capture your special moments! Below are some common questions and my investment details to help you get started.

What should I expect during a photo session?

Expect lots of fun and laughter! I aim to create a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere where you can be yourself and shine in every shot!

How do I book a session?

Booking is simple! Just head over to the Contact section, fill out the form, and I'll get back to you as soon as possible to discuss the details!

What should I wear for my session?

Wear something you feel comfortable and confident in! Solid colors and minimal patterns work best, but feel free to bring an outfit change or two!

How long does it take to receive my photos?

Typically, you'll receive your photos within three weeks of your session. I take care to edit each photo to perfection!

Do you travel for photo sessions?

Absolutely! I love exploring new places and am happy to travel within North Dakota and nearby areas. Let's discuss the details!

Can I bring props to the session?

Yes, please! Props can add a personal touch to your photos and make them even more special. Bring anything that reflects your personality or story!

What if it rains on the day of my outdoor session?

No worries! We can reschedule or find a lovely indoor location to continue with the session. Your comfort is my priority!
